Artist Statement

My goal for this website was to make it more of a general display of the materials that would lead to the different web pages with their own unique little backdrops and moving Parts in the background. Although I wanted to take a different approach when it came to the main page where you would be able to go into those different sections. I want to make it based on a menu that you would see in a video game as it's happily showing you the options that you can choose from and reacting generously to each option that you hover over or choose. I initially wanted to include more animation in the webpages and 2D Arts tabs and maybe add more tabs but i had to cut corners for the sake of time
